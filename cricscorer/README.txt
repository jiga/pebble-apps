This is my first pebble app, aimed for those who play Cricket. 

Features:
- Keeps track of Overs, Runs, Wickets and current Run Rate.
- Uses these events to compute score: 1 Run, 2 Run, 3 Runs, 4 Runs, 5 Runs, 6 Runs, 7 Runs, Ball, No Ball, Wide Ball, Out!, Undo.
- Down Button is used to cycle through list of these events: 1 Run, 2 Run, 3 Runs, 4 Runs, 5 Runs, 6 Runs, 7 Runs, No Ball, Wide Ball, Out!.
- Up Button is used for Undo event.
- Select Button used for incrementing Balls or selecting one of the events displayed by using Up or Down Button.
- Keeps history of last 12 events. 
- Reverse or Undo last 12 events, scores will go back accordingly.
- Keeps score of two innings (one each for Home team and Away team)
- Long press Select Button to toggle Innings.

All comments or suggestions are most welcome :)  
Hope you will enjoy this app as much as you enjoy playing Cricket!
